A Belief

February 14, 2023, 7:42 PM

I have always held a belief that is common in films, TV shows, and fiction works. However, to my surprise, my friend found it quite interesting when I shared it with them. This belief is that when faced with great difficulties and challenges, I do not need to panic because I know there is one who will definitely help me - my future self since I will not become her without going through all of this. She will have already read this part of the story. Even if she cannot physically travel back in time to guide me, she can accompany me calmly as we load the rest of the story together, so there is nothing to be afraid of as we face the future together. (I’ve always admired her, lol )

我一直有一个信念，其实在影视剧和文化作品中很常见，但出乎意料的是，我的朋友听了之后还是觉得很奇妙，这个信念就是：当面临巨大困难和挑战时，不用慌，因为我知道有一个人绝对会帮我，这个人就是未来的我自己 – 不经历这一切，我也不会成为她。作为已经「读完这段进度条」的人，即使她不能从物理上穿越回来给我指引，但精神上也是可以淡定从容地陪我往后加载，我们一起就没有什么惊恐，就是这样，我一直都好崇拜她的哈哈哈！
