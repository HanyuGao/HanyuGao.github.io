BQ Questions

May 6, 2022, 2:41 PM

Dive Deep

  • Tell me about a specific metric you used to identify a need for a change in your department. Did you create the metric, or was it already available? How did this and other information influence the change? What was the outcome of this change?
  • Tell me about a time when you were trying to understand a complex problem on your team, and you had to dig into the details to figure it out.
  • Tell me about a situation that required you to dig deep to get to the root cause.

Think Big

  • Tell me about a time you drove adoption for your vision/ideas.
  • Tell me about a time when you were working on an initiative or goal and saw an opportunity to do something much bigger or better than the initial focus

Be Curious

  • Tell me about a time when you realized you needed a deeper level of subject matter expertise to do your job well. What did you do about it? What was the outcome? Is there anything you would have done differently?
  • Tell me about a time when you didn’t know what to do next or how to solve a challenging problem
  • Describe a time when you took on work outside of your comfort area. How did you identify what you needed to learn to be successful? How did you go about building expertise to meet your goal? Did you meet your goal?

Customer Obsession

  • Tell me about a time when you went above and beyond for a customer. Why did you do it? How did the customer respond? What was the outcome?
  • Describe a difficult interaction you had with a customer.
