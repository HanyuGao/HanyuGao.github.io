My Vlogs

2022-11-15

I’ve always wanted to learn something new for fun and am interested in video editing. So I made a plan to post 5 vlogs every year since I didn’t like myself to be so rushed or transactional, which might ruin my passion as a beginner.

Four years passed, and I made 20 vlogs by CapCut or Final Cut Pro. 17 were in Chinese, and 3 were in English after I began to have new friends from other cultures. I had fun; surprisingly, my friends loved my vlogs, too.
