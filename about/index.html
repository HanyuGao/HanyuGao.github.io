Hi

I’m a Product Manager, but when I started working, I did marketing. Much data was like a swirling vortex that got me lost. I tried “surfing” on “waves” but found the issue was my “board” – the data dashboard is not easy to learn insights. Then I redesigned and proposed the analytical data layers and persuaded engineers to implement them. Then it hit my mind, why not become a PM? Then I pivoted to PM, which got me out of bed on Monday easier.

Over the 8 years of experience in product management, I dedicated myself to customers/users to see a new future out of existing software or from nowhere: I’ve created a first-of-its-kind tracker that lets 30K+ soccer players see their performance data on mobile. I’ve built an online community from scratch that increased DAU by 13%.

I enjoy learning new things and connecting with people across a range of industries, so don’t hesitate to reach out if you’d like to get in touch: tpm.lori@gmail.com
